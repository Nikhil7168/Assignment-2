STALWART COACHING-- HOW TO BE BRAVE AS A BASKETBALL COACH

A stalwart basketball coach is brave in his or her mind and also brave with his or her words.
A stalwart coach is a loyal coach and never throws players under the proverbial bus of critique or blame. A stalwart coach is a self-disciplined coach who always reframes the deficits into opportunities.

When you are learning to ride a motorcycle, you have to look past the turn in the road. Focusing on the turn will cause you to crash.
Looking past the curve keeps your focus on where you want to go. The same is true in coaching.
